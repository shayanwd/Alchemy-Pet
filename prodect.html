Who is this for?

Breeds prone to vision problems

  • Labs
  • Poodles
  • Dachshunds
  • Cocker Spaniels
  • Flat-faced breeds (pugs, shih tzus, bulldogs)

How to tell if your dog has vision problems

  • Bumping into things
  • Hesitation in low light or darkness
  • Cloudy, red, or swollen eyes
  • Excessive eye rubbing or pawing
  • Difficulty catching toys to treats
